GBV & HIV: Protecting Women and Girls

On World AIDS Day, Young and Gifted Youth Development Initiative (YAGYDI) highlights a critical reality: Gender-Based Violence increases women’s and girls’ vulnerability to HIV. Women and girls who experience violence often:

Cannot negotiate safe sex.

Face forced or coerced sexual activity.

Live with fear that prevents them from seeking testing or treatment.

Lack access to accurate information and supportive health services.

AI and Coding: The Future of Development

AI is transforming how we code, from automating repetitive tasks to suggesting smarter solutions. Tools like GitHub Copilot and ChatGPT help developers write code faster, debug efficiently, and explore new ideas.

While AI accelerates workflow, human creativity and problem-solving remain essential. The best developers use AI as a collaborative partner, enhancing their skills and building innovative, future-ready projects.

AI isn’t just a tool — it’s a partner that makes coding smarter and more efficient, helping developers focus on creativity and innovation.
